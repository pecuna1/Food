Eggs Benedict

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 English muffins
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 1 batch of Hollandaise sauce

Instructions:

  1. Toast the English muffins until they are golden brown.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a pan over medium heat. Place the ham slices in the pan and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until heated through.
  3. Fill a saucepan with about 2 inches of water and bring to a simmer. Crack the eggs into individual small bowls. Gently slide the eggs into the water and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the whites are set and the yolks are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  4. To assemble the Eggs Benedict, place a slice of ham on top of each toasted English muffin. Top each with a poached egg and a spoonful of Hollandaise sauce. Serve immediately.
